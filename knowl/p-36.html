An “OER coordinator” position for a SAC or for a collection of related SACs. For example the MTH SAC has a coordinator for online courses, for student advising, and for assessment; and these positions come with course release and defined responsibilities. A coordinator could manage OER-based SAC root D2L shells, print center order numbers, ISBNs, a faculty reference page, questions from faculty about all things OER, and executing some of the SAC’s needs for routine maintenance.
